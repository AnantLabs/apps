## About LimeSurvey

Build and customize your own web-based surveys with LimeSurvey. LimeSurvey has many features such as the ability to use multiple question types, survey branching, supports dozens of languages, public or private surveys and you can provide a token to ensure that people canâ€™t take a survey twice. LimeSurvey has basic reporting and analytic features to help you understand the results and you can export the results into Excel, CSV, SPSS and other formats.
